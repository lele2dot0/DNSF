About Us

The Dan Ngerem Sports Foundation is a privately motivated NGO with a selfless commitment to serve by creating enablement for the fulfilment of dreams especially in school age children and particularly in sports

We do this by donating sports equipment & educational items as well as the outright sponsorship of deserving students. We will always be there to lend a hand of support.
