Our mission

The Dan Ngerem Sports Foundation is a privately motivated NGO with a selfless commitment to serve by creating enablement for the fulfilment of dreams especially in school age children and particularly in sports. With the belief that a good and successful sports person must have a solid educational background, the Foundation also supports education as well. We do this by donating sports equipment & educational items as well as the outright sponsorship of deserving students. We will always be there to lend a hand of support.

What We Do

Fulfilling dreams of school age children in sports

donations

Donation of Sports equipment and educational items to schools.

Sponsorship

Outright Sponsorship of deserving students in school & sports.

advocacy

Advocacy for sporting programmes at all levels of education and at the professional and international level .
